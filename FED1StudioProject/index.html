About Us

New Horizon Training Center was founded in 2017 under the vision of Alice Cooperson. for a technical training center for students to get an affordable education in the newest forms of Information Technology (IT). Our mission statement is as follows: "Our goal is to educate the next wave of IT professionals and give them the skills to succeed in the fast-paces world of technology.”

We strive to give our students the knowledge and skills required for employment and career advancement in today’s competitive world of technology and will enable them to develop the professional skills and technical proficiency necessary in today’s job market. We strive to provide IT training and certifications in skills that will boost and promote an individual’s career and benefit the community they serve. In addition, we excel by increasing our awareness of the community’s IT needs and how to best address those needs by way of a comprehensive training regimen, input from community members and experts, hiring the most qualified staff, and committing to training in the most up to date technologies, with both in person and virtual sessions available. New Horizon distinguishes ourselves from other IT training facilities by setting high standards for our administrators, our faculty, and our students; this is achieved by developing sound policies and practices and standing firm on the principles we represent.

At New Horizon Training Centers, students receive so much more than an ordinary education. We pride ourselves on creating a unique, exciting environment that embraces all types of learners and promotes growth both academically and personally. Students and families are also welcomed into a dynamic and supportive community.

Interested in learning more about New Horizon Training Center? We invite you to keep exploring our website and get in touch with questions about our values, academics, community and more. Your new career is waiting. Remember, a new day begins at the Horizon. Join us today!
